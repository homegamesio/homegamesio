Privacy Policy

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform. By using Homegames, you agree to the terms of this policy.
What information we collect:
  • Email addresses: We collect your email address when you create an account on Homegames. We use your email address to identify your account and to communicate with you about your account, such as sending password reset emails and notifications about updates to our platform.
  • Anonymized platform usage data: We collect anonymized usage data to help us improve our platform and understand how our users are interacting with our platform. This data includes information such as client interactions, which games are being played, how long each game was played, and errors that occur. This data is not linked to any individual user and is used solely for analytical purposes.

How we protect your information:

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.
Your choices:
  • You can update or delete your account information at any time by logging into your account and making the desired changes.
  • You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails from us by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in the email.
