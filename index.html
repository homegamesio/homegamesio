Homegames is a free, open-source game platform

Play games built by the Homegames community or build your own

Users download and run the Homegames game server on their own computer, making every game on the Homegames platform instantly available to every device on the same wifi network.

Almost everything is built with JavaScript. We try to build as much as we reasonably can from scratch, and everything we build is open source. Check out our getting started wiki for more info. We'll have a lot more guides/documentation very soon!

At the moment, we have a few "games" that test input handling, rendering, performance, etc. but nothing really fully featured. Download the latest stable build, check out our public demo, or watch this video to get an idea of what we have so far.

Homegames is maintained by Joseph Garcia & Yazeed Loonat. We both work full-time as "professional" software developers and work on Homegames in our free time. We've been friends for most of our lives and we both love games. Our goal is to provide accessible tools for people to make what they want and have fun

For questions or comments, reach out to us on Twitter or Reddit, or send an email to joseph@homegames.io.

We have a weekly podcast where we talk about Homegames updates and what's going on in our lives. You can tweet questions to @homegamesio or email us at podcast@homegames.io to be featured on the podcast. We upload new videos every Wednesday!
